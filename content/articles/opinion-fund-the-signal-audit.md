If Metropolis wants a world-class grid, it needs a world-class apology when the grid hallucinates stop names in a dark tunnel.

The N-Line audit is not a column inch for a slow news day — it is a down payment on riders trusting the next alert when something real breaks.

City Hall should treat the independent engineers like election monitors: paid, protected, and impossible to fire by fax.
