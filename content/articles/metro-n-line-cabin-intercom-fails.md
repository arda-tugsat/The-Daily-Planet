Maintenance logs obtained by the Daily Planet show cabin intercom controllers on twelve N-Line trains received firmware builds intended for the older K-Series rolling stock.

Dispatchers told investigators they improvised handwritten sheets at the central rail desk after automated stop titles began drifting by one station during rush hour.

Union leaders demanded a full rollback before holiday convention traffic peaks; MTM officials said a staged patch window would begin Thursday at 2 a.m.
